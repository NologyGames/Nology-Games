Book

After giving some game development trainings in universities of Rio Grande do Sul state, in Brazil, the partners decided to put the trainings and their game development knowledge into a book named "Desenvolvimento de Jogos Eletrônicos - Teoria e Prática". It was published in Portuguese only, in the Brazilian market in March, 2005. The book was published by Novatec Editora.
