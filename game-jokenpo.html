Welcome to JoKenPo! Have you ever played "Paper Rock Scissors"? That's what it's all about. In this great game, featuring 3D graphics and funny characters, you are invited to beat all the enemies in a challenging maze, full of traps and special items that turn you and your enemies into Rock, Paper or Scissor. There is just one rule: Paper wraps Rock which breaks Scissors which cut Paper.

JoKenPo is designed for anyone who wants to start playing with Easy and Instant Fun! Whether you are a casual or a "full time" player, JoKenPo will deliver great enjoyment for your entertainment needs. Invite your friends and family and start playing!

Features

  • Funny 3D characters of Paper, Rock and Scissor
  • Dazzling 3D mazes full of challenges and surprises
  • 30 different levels where the difficulties increase progressively
  • Simple to play: use only direction keys on the keyboard or joystick
  • Easy to learn, difficult to master
  • 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium and Hard
  • Full Screen and colorful animated graphics
  • Engaging soundtrack
  • Keyboard and joystick support
  • Save/Load support

System Requirements

  • Pentium 233Mhz or higher;
  • 32Mb RAM
  • 20Mb free disk space
  • Windows 95/98/Me/2000 and XP;
  • 3D Card compatible with DirectX 8.0 or higher
