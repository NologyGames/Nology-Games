Koko Arena

Download Full Version Now!

Welcome to the wonderful world of Koko Arena! Enjoy the Kokos, funny and appealing characters that represent sixteen countries. The game is similar to volleyball, but you have to use the Koko's head to hit the ball. The rule is simple: don't let the ball fall on your side of the playing field! And there is a championship, where you will have to beat all the Kokos, thus you can win the coveted KokoCup trophy.

Features

  • Innovative gameplay
  • Simple to play: only mouse is required
  • Two game types: Quick Game or Championship
  • Championship with 15 matches
  • An extremely competitive and tense match is created
  • Power-ups that can help or handicap your shot, making the match much funnier and challenging
  • 16 appealing and hilarious characters representing countries
  • Full screen graphics
  • 7 different and amazing arenas
  • Two player mode lets you take on a friend
  • Matches of three sets with 7 points each
  • User-friendly interface
  • Joystick and keyboard support
  • Save/Load support

System Requirements

  • Windows 95/98/ME/NT/2000/XP
  • DirectX 3.0 or higher
  • Processor 266 MHz or higher
  • 32 MB RAM
  • 17 MB free disk space
